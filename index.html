Fascinating Cat Facts

🍭

Taste

Cats can't taste sweetness because they don't have test receptors for it.

🐾

Jump Skills

Cats are one of the few mammals that can activate 100% of their muscles when they jump.

👂

Listening Skills

Cats are likely smarter than dogs because they possess extrordinarily hearing that is better than dogs hearing.

🛏️

Cat naps

Cats sleep 70% of their lives.

🌳

Relatives

All cats, big and small, belong to the felidae family.

🐈

Flexibility

Cats actually have more vertibrae in them than most mammals. Their vertebrae also have especially elastic cushioning discs between them, so their spines are incredibly flexible. This flexibility helps make the cat the fastest animal on land.
